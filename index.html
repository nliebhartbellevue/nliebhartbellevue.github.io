My Creative Process

All of my development, UI/UX and design projects are based off of a practiced formula to get the result that I am looking for.

1

Discuss The Project

To get a clear picture of what you need I start every project by receiving an initial brief of what you want. Having this gives me enough context to properly provide you with a proposal and ultimately tailor my services to your project goals.

1

Prototype & Design

Having a good set of wireframes and prototypes is the bedrock of any successful project. This also allows me to better understand your vision while always making sure your users are the primary focus. Next, I turn concept into design, by giving you a modern and clean working layout.

1

Development

When the coding begins there will generally be less communication from me as this is quite an involved process that doesn't require much feedback. This is where I take the visuals of your project and make them into something real that your users will love.

1

Training & Launch

After the website has been coded but before it goes live I like to provide in-depth training. When it's time to launch the new site/application I'll take care of everything. All you'll need to do is prepare the champaign for the party!
